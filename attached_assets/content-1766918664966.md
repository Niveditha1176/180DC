# Vellore Institute of Technology

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63c0930a7ee820653e9b5c84/6762cb1545de6e92e5fa8da4_vitc.webp)

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/63b610d81215b25001c51b2b/63c880e75a0123f3fc322f7c_tamplate-page-bg.svg)

Welcome to the 180 Degrees Consulting branch at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Chennai!

We are proud to be the first 180 Degrees Consulting branch in VIT.

Established on Jan 16 2024, we tap into the dynamic spirit of Chennai, a megacity teeming with social innovation. Chennai boasts a thriving social sector with over 900 NGOs and social enterprises, making it the perfect launchpad for our mission.

At 180DC VITC, we empower our student consultants to explore their careers in social impact consulting as well as bridge the gap between theory and practice by offering real-world consulting experiences.

Our diverse membership, spanning engineering , business, and law students fosters a collaborative environment where our consultants learn from a multitude of perspectives.

Do check out our work and get to know about how students from diverse domains come together to make a difference through consulting!

### Benefits to joining this branch

1

#### Real world consulting experience

180DC VITC offers you the chance to dive headfirst into the world of consulting. You'll work on real-life projects for non-profit organizations and social enterprises, tackling critical challenges they face. This hands-on experience allows you to develop and refine essential consulting skills, including problem-solving, analysis, communication, and client relationship management. You'll leave the program with a tangible portfolio that showcases your capabilities to future employers.

2

#### Access a Global Network and Resources

As part of the extensive 180 Degrees Consulting network, you'll gain access to a wealth of resources that extend far beyond your local chapter. Connect with a global community of experienced consultants, tap into a library of case studies and best practices, and benefit from the collective knowledge of the organization. This global network provides invaluable support and opens doors to potential future opportunities.

3

#### Foster Cross-disciplinary Collaboration: Learn from Diverse Perspectives

180DC VITC brings together a dynamic team of students from various academic backgrounds, including business, economics, social sciences, and more. This interdisciplinary environment fosters a collaborative spirit where you can learn from each other's unique perspectives and skillsets. By working together on projects, you'll gain a well-rounded understanding of complex issues and develop the ability to effectively collaborate in diverse teams â€“ a crucial skill for today's workplace.

4

#### Make a Measurable Social Impact

At 180DC VITC, your consulting work goes beyond theory. You'll directly contribute to positive social change by tackling real-world problems faced by non-profit organizations and social enterprises. Witnessing the tangible impact of your work is an incredibly rewarding experience that will fuel your passion for making a difference in the world.
